www.43oh.com is a community of hobbyist and enthusiasts with interest in the MSP430 microcontroller from Texas Instruments, as well as the other MCU families in the TI portfolio. You can find an active community interested in helping each other answer questions and collaborate on projects. Use the forums to double check your hardware designs, guage and drum up interest for you hardware projects, and find out tips and tricks to improve your features and performance.
